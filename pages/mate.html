Mate

Ingredients

  • Water.
  • Yerba mate .
  • Sugar if needed.

Steps

  1. Heat water to 75-85°C (167-185°F).
  2. Add the yerba mate to about 3/4 of the mate.
  3. Sweeten your drink by adding a bit of sugar if you want to.
  4. Tilt your mate so that the yereba mate is in a 45° angle.
  5. Pour the hot water on the lower point of the yerba.
  6. Add the metal straw through the wet yerba.
  7. Enjoy!
