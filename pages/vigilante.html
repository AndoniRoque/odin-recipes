Postre Vigilante

Ingredientes

  • Cheese
  • Sweetpotato Jam or Quince Jelly

Steps

  1. Cut 1/2 inch slices of a soft cheese, not too salty!
  2. Cut another 1/2 inch slice of Sweetpotato Jam or Quince Jelly
  3. Put one on top of the other (usually the cheese goes underneath the sweet) and enjoy!
